The primary aim of this portal is providing citizens with a centralized platform where any citizen of the state can lodge his/ her grievances to the respective departments. Through Rajasthan Sampark, Government of Rajasthan intends to achieve all important aspects of good governance viz. Robust Delivery System, Transparency, Communication, Grievance Redressal and Sensitivity towards citizens.
A quick glance at our services:
  1. State level Call Center with integrated web portal acts as a single point of contact for addressing and redressing various citizen centric queries and grievances related to government services.
  2. Facility to register a complaint without being physically present at the respective department.
  3. Free of cost registration of complaints at the Panchayat and District Level through Rajasthan Sampark centers.
  4. Free of cost call center services available 24X7 to record complaints at the toll free number: 18001806127
  5. Facility to download “native” app for smartphone users.
  6. In the case that a registered complaint is not acted on, facility for a personal hearing with the concerned department officials on the allotted Thursday of each month.
    • The panchayat samiti level Rajasthan Sampark centers can be contacted on first Thursday of each month under the supervision of the sub-divisional officer
    • If the individual is not satisfied with the panchayat samiti level hearing, he/she may contact the district level Rajasthan Sampark centers.
    • If the individual is not satisfied with the district level hearing, the matter might be taken up with the concerned department authorities at the state level.
